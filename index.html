Thai Airways International plc is the flag carrier airline of Thailand.Formed in 1961 as a joint venture between SAS and Thai Airways Company, the airline has its corporate headquarters in Vibhavadi Rangsit Road, Chatuchak district, Bangkok,and primarily operates from Suvarnabhumi Airport.
