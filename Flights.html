Thai Airways serves destinations across the Asia-Pacific region, Europe, and Australia, with domestic flights within Thailand.

Examples of Destinations Served. Domestic Thailand: Flights are available to cities such as Chiang Mai, Phuket, and Hat Yai. Asia-Pacific: Destinations in this region include Tokyo, Seoul, Singapore, and Osaka. Europe: Thai Airways serves major cities in Europe, including London, Frankfurt, and Copenhagen. Australia: Flights are available to destinations like Melbourne.
